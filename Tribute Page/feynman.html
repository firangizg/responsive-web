Richard Phillips Feynman

Here are some facts about Richard Feynman.

  • He was an actor in the movie "Anti Clock" in a proffesor role.
  • Feynman was an art lover and he signed his paintings with the name "Ofey"
  • With his playful personality, he was famous as a prankster in Los Almos
  • His last words before death were "I would hate to die twice. It is so boring".
  • He was relieved to discover that Santa Claus was not real as a child, since the reality was easier than understanding how many children got that much gifts.
  • He was born in New York city.
  • He received his bachelor's degree from MIT.
